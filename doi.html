Usability Testing
Interviews were conducted with 6 of the survey participants ranged in age from 30- 45. All came from varying backgrounds and shared an interest in giving back to society and nature. The participants were asked to do the following tasks: - Try to find public lands and information you would need to go there and utilize for a purpose such as camping or recreation. - Search for Volunteer events for National Parks. - Understand basic usage/functionality of DOI website. Find something of interest and pursue it as an actionable event.
